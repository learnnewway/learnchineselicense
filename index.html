Operational parameters and telemetry for the Chinese driving theory exam.

Critical Operating Parameters

Speed Vectors

  • Urban (No center line): Max 30 km/h.
  • Urban (One lane per direction): Max 50 km/h.
  • Highway (No center line): Max 40 km/h.
  • Highway (One lane per direction): Max 70 km/h.
  • Expressway limits: Min 60 km/h, Max 120 km/h.

Right-of-Way Logic

  • Always yield to the RIGHT at uncontrolled intersections.
  • Turning vehicles yield to straight-moving vehicles.
  • Right-turning vehicles yield to left-turning vehicles entering the same vector.
  • Going uphill? The uphill vehicle has priority, unless the downhill vehicle is already halfway down.

The 12-Point Structural Limit

Accumulating 12 points in a single cycle results in immediate license suspension. Memorize these critical failures.

12-Point Fatalities (Instant Fail)

DUI, fleeing an accident, driving without a plate, driving a mismatched vehicle class, speeding >50% over limit, driving backwards on an expressway.

6-Point Major Errors

Running a red light, illegal occupation of the expressway emergency lane, speeding 20%-50% over limit, driving with a suspended license.

3 & 2-Point Infractions

3 Pts: Failing to yield at a crosswalk, speeding <20% over limit. 2 Pts: Using a phone, no seatbelt, parking violations.

Exam Psychology & Hacks

The "Chinglish" Filter

The English translation is notoriously clunky. If a question seems illogical, think about the literal Chinese translation. "Violating the provisions" just means breaking a rule.

The Golden Rule of Safety

If an answer choice contains the words "rapidly", "immediately", "accelerate", or "forcefully", it is almost ALWAYS wrong. The correct answer is usually the one that mentions "decelerate", "yield", or "observe safely".

Accident Logic

If there is an accident with no injuries, you must take photos, MOVE the car to avoid blocking traffic, and then negotiate. If there are injuries, DO NOT move the vehicles. Rescue the injured first.

Preparation Tools

Do not take the exam without practicing the exact bank of 1,500 questions. Download Laowai Drive or Drive in China and run mock tests until you consistently hit 95+.
