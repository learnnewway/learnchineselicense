First Aid Protocols

Arterial Bleeding: Blood is bright red and spurts. Apply pressure closer to the heart.

Venous Bleeding: Blood is dark red and flows continuously. Apply pressure away from the heart.

Fractures & Burns: NEVER attempt to reset a bone. Spray burns with cold water. Do NOT pop blisters or peel clothing.

Mechanical Variables

ABS Braking: Press the pedal down firmly and hold it. Never pump ABS brakes.

Tire Blowout: Hold steering wheel firmly, release accelerator, brake gently. NEVER slam the brakes.

Fog & Aquaplaning: Turn on fog/hazard lights (never high beams). Release the accelerator immediately if aquaplaning.
