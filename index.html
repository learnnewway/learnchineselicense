Subject 1 Exam Guide

License Conversion

Pass Requirement: 90/100

The Revocation Matrix (Bans)

Fleeing a fatal accident (constituting a crime) LIFETIME BAN
DUI causing a major traffic accident LIFETIME BAN
Drunk driving a commercial vehicle 10 YEAR BAN
Drunk driving (Standard DUI) causing accident 5 YEAR BAN
Fraud/Cheating to obtain license 3 YEAR BAN

Fines Database

20 - 200 RMB

Illegal parking, minor continuous infractions where driver refuses to move.

200 - 2,000 RMB

Driving without a license, speeding >50%, fleeing a non-criminal accident.

2,000 - 5,000 RMB

Using forged or altered license plates, certificates, or driving licenses.

Point Deduction Ledger

12 Points

  • DUI (Drinking and driving).
  • Driving wrong way / reversing on expressway.
  • Using fake/forged license plates.
  • Speeding >50% on expressway.

9 Points

  • Deliberately obscuring license plates.
  • Illegal parking on a city expressway.
  • Driving a mismatched vehicle class.
  • Driving a vehicle without a plate.

6 Points

  • Running a red traffic light.
  • Occupying the emergency lane.
  • Failing to yield to a school bus.
  • Driving with a suspended license.

3 Points

  • Failing to yield at crosswalks.
  • Speeding <20% over limit on expressways.
  • Making a call / holding phone while driving.

First Aid Protocols

Arterial Bleeding: Blood is bright red and spurts. Apply pressure closer to the heart.

Venous Bleeding: Blood is dark red and flows. Apply pressure away from the heart.

Fractures: NEVER attempt to reset a bone. Keep the casualty immobilized and splint in place.

Burns: Spray with cold water. Do NOT pop blisters or peel clothing stuck to the skin.

Mechanical Variables

ABS Braking: Press the pedal down firmly and hold it. Never pump ABS brakes.

Tire Blowout: Hold steering wheel firmly, release accelerator, brake gently. NEVER slam the brakes.

Fog Protocol: Turn on fog and hazard lights. NEVER use high beams (blinds the driver).

Aquaplaning: Release the accelerator immediately. Keep the steering straight.
