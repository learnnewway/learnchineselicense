Police Hand Signals (Override all electronic lights)

Stop Signal Left arm raised straight up, palm facing forward. ALL traffic must stop.

Straight Ahead Left arm raised sideways, right arm sweeps across chest. Proceed straight.

Left Turn Right arm points to the right, left arm sweeps forward. Left-turning traffic may proceed.

Pull Over Left arm raised straight up, right arm points down and sweeps horizontally. Pull over to the right and stop.

Memorize the arm angles. The left arm usually indicates the target traffic, the right arm indicates the action.

Prohibition Signs

Shape/Color: White background, red circle, black symbol (often with a red slash).

Logic: "Do Not Do This."

  • No Entry
  • No Parking
  • No Honking

Warning Signs

Shape/Color: Yellow triangle, black border, black symbol.

Logic: "Hazard Ahead, Slow Down."

  • Sharp Curve Ahead
  • Pedestrian Crossing Ahead
  • Village/Town Ahead

Mandatory Signs

Shape/Color: Blue circle, white symbol.

Logic: "You MUST Do This."

  • Go Straight Only
  • Turn Left Only
  • Honk Horn
